About

We are a Portland Oregon based Design-Build company taking on residential and commercial projects for 2016.

  • 1990

    Our Humble Beginnings

    In 1990, founder Taka Nakagawa moved to Portland Oregon from Nagoya, Japan and began working for a building supply company exporting buidling materials overseas.

  • April 2004

    Sakura is Born

    We are now running and staffing multiple large projects for both commercial and residential applications, the ball is rolling. Sakura has become a well established design-build company with many years still ahead.

  • December 2006

    Exciting Housing Infill Project

    We salvage and move a historic 1890's home to a new location in the Irvington neighborhood earning us Irvington's Best Housing Infill Project award.

  • January 2012

    Bridge Over the Ocean

    Sakura begins building relationships with builders and archtiects in Japan who do business in the North West, by offering consulting services. We still do to this day.
