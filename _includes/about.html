About

We are a Portland Oregon based Design-Build company taking on residential and commercial projects of all sizes.

  • 1990

    Our Humble Beginnings

    In 1990, founder Taka Nakagawa moved to Portland Oregon from Nagoya Japan and began working as a carpenter remodeling old homes in the Irvington and Laurelhurst areas.

  • April 2004

    Sakura is Born

    By now it was time to take an official name. Now caring for and staffing multiple large projects in both commercial and residential applications, the ball was definately rolling. Sakura has been realized.

  • January 2012

    Bridge Over the Ocean

    Finding more time to branch out over some years of growth, Sakura begins building relationships with builders and archtiects in Japan who do business in the North West, and begins offering consulting services.

  • May 2015

    Rebirth

    After a brief hiatus, and much surfing, and with the introduction of new talent, Sakura is refreshed and plugging itself back in to the Portland market.
